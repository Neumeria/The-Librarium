• Command Supremacy + Battle Supremacy = Ranged attacks have chance to outright kill infantry.

  • Command Supremacy + Tactical Supremacy = Melee damage bonus.

  • Battle Supremacy + Tactical Supremacy = Knockback immunity.


  • Command Supremacy's effect reduces the energy cost of abilities of nearby squads. This effect lasts until the end of mission deployment, allowing the effect to stack with itself. After a few stacks of Command Supremacy, it is possible for even the heaviest energy-consuming abilities to be cast at almost no cost.
